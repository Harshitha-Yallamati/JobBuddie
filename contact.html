Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about JobBuddy

How accurate are the AI career recommendations?

Our AI analyzes thousands of data points including your skills, experience, interests, and current job market trends. Our recommendations have an 85% accuracy rate based on user feedback and successful career transitions.

Is JobBuddy free to use?

Yes! JobBuddy offers a comprehensive free tier that includes resume analysis, career recommendations, and basic skill development plans. Premium features like personalized mentorship and advanced analytics are available with our paid plans.

How long does the resume analysis take?

Our AI typically processes and analyzes your resume within 2-3 minutes. The analysis includes skill extraction, experience evaluation, and personalized career path recommendations.

Can I update my profile and get new recommendations?

Absolutely! You can update your resume, interests, and goals at any time. Our AI will generate fresh recommendations based on your updated information and the latest job market data.

Do you offer job placement services?

While we don't directly place candidates, we partner with leading companies and job boards to connect you with relevant opportunities. Our platform also provides interview preparation and networking guidance.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
